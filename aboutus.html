How It Started ?


The founders are students from HR Institute of Science & Technology. We all are from Information Technological University. Once we all were just attending college classes, just doing our routine work. All of a sudden a message comes in our WA group aboud GDSC (Google Developer Student Club) competition. We instantly made up a team and started thinking of what topic to choose. Then one day a thought came up that why not do something to save the most crucial drink that we require on day-to-day basis.

But we had to think of some idea that would benefit people and encourage them to save water. Then we introduces the money angle. If we are able to save people's money then they'll definitely save water. That's how distil was born.
